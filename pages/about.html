About AdFreeCooking

In 202X, finding recipes online is a total nightmare:

  • Recipe sites take ages to load
  • The recipes themselves are buried in blog content and ads
  • The sites have intrusive popups and cookie requests

I hate this experience. It frustrates me to no end.
So, I made this place; hope you like it!
